Sunflower

It is a beautiful flower and it is full of life

Sunflowers are well-known for being a happy flower and the perfect bloom for a summer flower delivery to brighten someone's mood!

"Sunflowers are a symbol of happiness and optimism. They represent longevity, love, and loyalty"

Did you know that sunflowers are very fast-growing plants? In fact, it requires only around 100 days from planting to reach maturity.

Sunflower Facts That Are So Sweet

  1. Not All Sunflowers Are Yellow
  2. Sunflowers Can Range in Height
  3. Young Sunflowers Track the Sun
  4. Mature Sunflowers Face East

Benefits of Sunflower

  • Sunflower Oil Has an Anti-Inflammatory Effect
  • Sunflower Oils Can Reduce Cholesterol Levels
  • Sunflowers Can Be Processed Into Sunbutter




Characteristic Sunflower
Plant Family Asteraceae
Appearance Large, bright yellow petals arranged in a spiral pattern around a dark center
Height Can range from a few feet to over 10 feet depending on the variety
Habitat Native to North and South America, widely cultivated worldwide
Flowering Season Summer to early fall
Uses Ornamental, oil production, birdseed
